Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna is made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. Perfect for family dinners or gatherings.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna noodles.
  2. Cook ground beef and add tomato sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, cheese, and meat sauce.
  4. Bake in oven for 45 minutes at 375Â°F.
