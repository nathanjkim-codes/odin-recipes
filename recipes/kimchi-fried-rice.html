Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Kimchi Fried Rice (Kimchi Bokkeumbap) is a simple and flavorful Korean dish made with day-old rice and aged kimchi.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Aged kimchi
  • Sesame oil
  • Gochujang (optional)
  • Green onions
  • Fried egg (optional)

Steps

  2. Add rice and mix well.
  3. Season with gochujang or soy sauce.
  4. Top with green onions and fried egg.
