Pancakes

Description

Fluffy, classic pancakes made from scratch. Perfect with maple syrup, butter, or fruits for a warm breakfast.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk in milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Heat a pan and pour batter.
  4. Flip when bubbles appear. Cook both sides until golden.
